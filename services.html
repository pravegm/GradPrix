GradPrix MBA Admissions Services Overview

What services does GradPrix offer?

  • Tailored Consulting Packages: Customized end-to-end support based on your specific needs, timeline, and budget. Includes school selection, essay coaching, resume optimization, recommendation strategy, interview preparation, and application form review.
  • Essay Coaching: In-depth brainstorming sessions, multiple rounds of detailed feedback, school-specific strategy, and final polish/proofreading.
  • Interview Preparation: School-specific mock interviews, behavioral question preparation, video review and feedback, follow-up strategy coaching.
  • Resume Review: Complete resume restructuring, achievement quantification, MBA-specific formatting with two rounds of revisions.
  • School Selection Strategy: Comprehensive profile analysis, school fit assessment, admissions probability analysis, customized school list.
  • Profile Assessment: Comprehensive profile review, strengths/weaknesses analysis, competitive positioning, improvement recommendations.
  • Recommendation Strategy: Recommender selection strategy, talking points development, story alignment coaching.

How much does it cost? Pricing varies based on services needed and schools targeted. Free 30-minute initial consultation available.
