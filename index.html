What is GradPrix? GradPrix is a premier MBA admissions consulting firm helping ambitious professionals gain admission to top-tier business schools worldwide. Founded by INSEAD alumni, they specialize in personalized guidance for candidates targeting top global MBA programs.

Key Facts:

  • 50+ successful admits to top MBA programs
  • $500,000+ in scholarships secured for clients
  • Clients from 20+ countries worldwide
  • INSEAD alumni-led consulting team
  • Free 30-minute initial consultation

Services Offered:

  • Comprehensive MBA Consulting Packages (end-to-end support)
  • Essay Coaching (compelling narrative development)
  • Interview Preparation (mock interviews with feedback)
  • Resume Review (strategic positioning)
