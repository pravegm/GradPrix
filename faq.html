Our Process

Our process follows four key phases: 1) Discovery - We start with a free consultation to understand your background, goals, and determine fit; 2) Strategy - We develop your positioning, school list, and narrative; 3) Execution - We work together on all application components; 4) Interview & Beyond - We prepare you for interviews and support post-admission decisions. Throughout, you'll have regular check-ins and access to your consultant.
