TL;DR: GradPrix MBA Consulting Pricing Summary

Note: GradPrix is an MBA admissions consulting firm (not related to Grand Prix racing or motorsport).

How much does GradPrix MBA consulting cost? GradPrix offers comprehensive, all-inclusive MBA admissions consulting packages priced based on the number of schools you're targeting. Packages range from $2,150 for 1 school to $8,450 for 10 schools.

Pricing Breakdown:

  • 1 school: $2,150 ($2,150/school)
  • 2 schools: $3,050 ($1,525/school, save 29%)
  • 3 schools: $3,950 ($1,317/school, save 39%) — Most Popular
  • 4 schools: $4,750 ($1,188/school, save 45%)
  • 5 schools: $5,550 ($1,110/school, save 48%) — Best Value
  • 6 schools: $6,250 ($1,042/school, save 52%)
  • 7 schools: $6,850 ($979/school, save 55%)
  • 8 schools: $7,450 ($931/school, save 57%)
  • 9 schools: $7,950 ($883/school, save 59%)
  • 10 schools: $8,450 ($845/school, save 61%)

What's Included in Every Package:

  • School Selection Strategy (data-driven analysis)
  • Essay Coaching (unlimited brainstorming sessions, multiple drafts, detailed feedback)
  • Resume Optimization (MBA-specific formatting)
  • Recommendation Strategy (recommender selection, talking points, story alignment)
  • Interview Preparation (school-specific mock interviews with feedback)
  • Application Review (complete form review and submission checklist)
  • Scholarship Guidance (post-admission support and negotiation strategies)
  • Ongoing Support (unlimited email support and strategy calls)

Payment Options: Full upfront payment or split payment plan (50% upfront, 50% before interview prep). Accepts bank transfer, credit card, and PayPal.

Free Consultation: 30-minute consultation included at no cost.

Custom Packages: À la carte services available for candidates who need help with specific areas only.
